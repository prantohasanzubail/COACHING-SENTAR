Admissions

SSC Batch

Admission Fee: 1000 BDT

Our SSC batch admission is open for students who are preparing for their Secondary School Certificate (SSC) examinations. With experienced teachers and comprehensive study materials, we aim to help students excel in their exams.

HSC Batch

Admission Fee: 1500 BDT

Our HSC batch admission is suitable for students aspiring to clear their Higher Secondary Certificate (HSC) examinations. We provide specialized coaching and personalized support to ensure academic success.

University Admission Batch

Admission Fee: 2000 BDT

Our university admission batch is designed for students preparing for admission tests to various universities. We offer comprehensive coaching, practice tests, and guidance to help students secure admission in their desired institutions.

Specialized Courses

Admission Fee: Varies

We also offer specialized courses for competitive exams such as BCS, Medical & Engineering admission tests, and language proficiency tests. The admission fee for these courses may vary based on the duration and curriculum.
