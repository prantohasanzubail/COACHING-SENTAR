Facilities

Classrooms

Our modern classrooms are designed to facilitate interactive learning. Each classroom is equipped with multimedia facilities and comfortable seating arrangements to ensure a conducive learning environment.

  • Interactive whiteboards for engaging lessons
  • Comfortable seating with individual desks
  • Audio-visual equipment for multimedia presentations
  • Ample natural light and ventilation
  • Flexible seating arrangements for group activities
  • Accessible for students with disabilities

Computer Lab

Our computer lab provides students with access to the latest technology and software applications. Whether it's programming, research, or multimedia projects, students can utilize the computer lab for various academic purposes.

  • High-speed internet connectivity
  • Desktop computers with essential software installed
  • Printing and scanning facilities
  • Technical support staff available for assistance
  • 24/7 access for enrolled students
  • Secure data storage and backup facilities

Library

Our library offers a vast collection of reference materials, textbooks, and academic resources to support students in their studies. It provides a quiet and conducive space for research, study, and self-directed learning.

  • Extensive collection of books, journals, and periodicals
  • Access to online databases and e-resources
  • Study tables and comfortable seating
  • Librarian assistance for research and reference
  • Quiet zones for focused study
  • Group study rooms with collaborative technology

Cafeteria

Our cafeteria offers a variety of nutritious meals and refreshments to keep students energized throughout the day. It provides a social space for students to relax, socialize, and enjoy healthy food options.

  • Freshly prepared meals and snacks
  • Comfortable seating area with a pleasant ambiance
  • Healthy food options catering to diverse dietary preferences
  • Clean and hygienic environment
  • Vending machines for snacks and beverages
  • Outdoor seating area for enjoying meals in the open air
